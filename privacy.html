Privacy Policy

OverPair Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 22, 2026

OverPair respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how OverPair handles your data.

Information OverPair Stores

OverPair stores the following information you enter into the app:

  • Poker session data
  • Opponent information
  • Bankroll data
  • Games, stakes, and locations
  • Session defaults and app preferences

How Your Data Is Stored

The information you enter is used only to provide the app’s features, including tracking sessions, viewing reports, and organizing poker-related records.

When Data Leaves Your Device

Your data only leaves your device if you choose to export your session data as a JSON file. OverPair does not automatically share, transmit, or sync your data.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

OverPair does not use third-party analytics tools. OverPair does not use third-party crash reporting tools.

Third-Party Services

OverPair does not sell your data and does not share your data with advertisers or data brokers. If you choose to contact support using the email address provided in the app, any information you include in your email will be handled through your email provider.

Data Retention and Control

Because your data is stored locally on your device, you control it. You may remove your data by deleting sessions, adjustments, opponents, and other app content within OverPair. You may also use the Reset Data feature to erase app data and start over.

Children’s Privacy

OverPair is not directed to children under 13, and the app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be made available within the app.

Contact
